# `logbook/` — licence

The `logbook/` folder mixes two cd-rules §0.3 layers.

## Templates — Coastline

`logbook/_templates/` content (e.g. `gate-closure.md`) is **Coastline**: it provides reusable forms for governance entries.

**Licence:** CC-BY-SA-4.0 — see [`/LICENSES/CC-BY-SA-4.0.txt`](../LICENSES/CC-BY-SA-4.0.txt).

## Entries — Sail

Logbook entries (`logbook/YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>.md`) are **Sail**: they are case analyses of governance decisions — interpretive deliberation records whose meaning depends on the epistemic commitments of Council-3 governance.

**Licence:** CC-BY-NC-SA-4.0 — see [`/LICENSES/CC-BY-NC-SA-4.0.txt`](../LICENSES/CC-BY-NC-SA-4.0.txt).

NonCommercial reflects cd-rules §0.3: interpretive work is protected from commercial repackaging without the epistemic context that produced it.

Both layers carry ShareAlike — derivatives must remain open under the same licence.
