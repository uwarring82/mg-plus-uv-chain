## Lock–Key separation

Per Council-3 standing doctrine: this Charter and its derived documents establish *locks* — stable concepts, structures, and protocols. Interpretations and applications by users of this framework are *keys* — free to vary, with authority derived from use rather than endorsement. The Charter does not control downstream interpretation; it provides a coordinate chart against which interpretations can be checked for consistency.
