## Why this paper

Identified in the first scout pass as **proof that BBO-at-280-class systems were normal in Mg ion labs before Friedenauer 2006**. The paper describes a single-mode CW dye laser frequency doubled in a 5-mm BBO crystal in an external bow-tie cavity, designed around 560 nm but used at Mg / Ca photoionization wavelengths; the Mg cooling transition at 280 nm is driven by a nearly identical laser system. Useful as a Q2 operational-application source; no lifetime number was visible in the first-pass abstract scout.

3. Whether 280 nm operation is direct or via 560 nm intermediate.

5. Any operational-lifetime or maintenance notes.
