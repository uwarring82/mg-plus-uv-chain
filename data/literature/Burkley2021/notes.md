## Why this paper

Identified in the third scout pass as the **most directly transferable CW deep-UV intracavity mirror lifetime data** for `KD-UV280-008` and `KD-UV280-012`. Reports oxide (HfO₂ / Al₂O₃ on SiO₂) coating degradation in UHV at <0.67 mbar O₂ with intracavity power ~5 W within minutes, and fluoride (MgF₂ / LaF₃ on CaF₂) coating survival at 10-16 W intracavity for hours. UV-ozone cleaning revives oxide mirrors. Closest published wavelength is ~244 nm (Yb-quadrupling), not 280 nm — but the oxygen-depletion / hydrocarbon-contamination mechanism is wavelength-scalable and the operating-power range overlaps the Friedenauer-class envelope.

6. UV-ozone cleaning protocol and recovery factor for oxide coatings.
7. Authors' mechanism statement (oxygen-depletion vs photodecomposition vs hydrocarbon polymerisation).
8. Any direct comparison to Gallego2017 thesis or other UHV / deep-UV cavity precedents.
